The Origin of Batman

Batman first appeared in the comic book scene in 1939 within Bob Kane's 'Detective Comics #27,' but his initial depiction differed greatly from his iconic image today. Originally, he sported a vibrant red suit with two wings and entered scenes swinging on a rope. It was Bill Finger who later introduced the alter ego Bruce Wayne and transformed Batman into the character we are familiar with today.
